Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026  ·  Version 1.0

⚠ Academic Project Notice ThriftHub is a web application developed as a final project for the course Advanced Dynamic Web Development (6AWEB) at Holy Angel University, Angeles City, Philippines. This platform is not a real commercial website and does not process actual payments, ship real goods, or store personal data beyond what is required for academic demonstration purposes. All content, products, and user accounts on this site are fictional and for educational use only.

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ThriftHub handles any information collected during the use of this web application. Because ThriftHub is a student-built academic prototype and not a commercial platform, the scope of data collection is minimal and limited strictly to what is necessary for demonstrating the application's features.

By using ThriftHub, you acknowledge that this is a school project developed at Holy Angel University and that no real commercial data practices apply.

2. Information We Collect

When you create an account, ThriftHub collects your full name, email address, and a hashed version of your password. This information is stored in a development database solely for the purpose of demonstrating user authentication functionality.

We do not collect payment information, government-issued IDs, or any other sensitive personal data. Any product browsing and cart activity is session-based and used only for demonstration.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively to:

Authenticate users during login and registration, simulate personalized features such as order history and user profiles, and fulfill the functional requirements of the 6AWEB course project at Holy Angel University.

Your data will not be used for marketing, advertising, analytics platforms, or any purpose outside of academic evaluation.

4. Data Sharing

ThriftHub does not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Data stored in the development database is accessible only to the project team members — Arita, Manaloto, Quiambao, and Valencia — for the purpose of academic evaluation and debugging.

5. Data Storage & Security

Account passwords are hashed before storage using industry-standard practices. However, because this is a student project running in a development environment, we strongly recommend not using real passwords or any credentials you use on other platforms.

The development database may be wiped at any time as part of the project lifecycle. Do not rely on ThriftHub to retain your data long-term.

6. Cookies

ThriftHub may use browser session storage to maintain your login state across page navigation. No third-party tracking cookies or analytics scripts are used in this application.

7. Your Rights

Since this is an academic project, formal data subject rights (such as GDPR or Data Privacy Act of 2012 requests) do not formally apply. However, if you would like your account or data removed from the development database, you may contact the project team through the Contact page.

8. Contact

For any questions about how this project handles data, please reach out through the Contact page or contact the School of Computing at Holy Angel University, Angeles City, Pampanga, Philippines.
