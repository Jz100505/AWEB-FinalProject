Our Mission

Thrift shopping
the way it should be.

"The Philippines has millions of thrift lovers — students, young professionals, budget-conscious families — all scrolling endlessly through chaotic social media pages just to find a ₱200 jacket. We said enough."

ThriftHub was built to solve a real Filipino problem: the ukay-ukay experience is beloved, but the process of actually buying online was broken. No organized listings, no size filters, no reliable availability info — just DMs and crossed fingers.

We built the platform we always wished existed. A clean, fast, honest marketplace where every item has a photo, a price, a size, and a story.

How We Got Here

The ThriftHub Story.

The Problem

The Scroll That Never Ends

Every Filipino thrift buyer knows the pain — scrolling through hundreds of Facebook posts, DMing sellers who ghost you, never knowing if your size is available. The ukay-ukay culture is rich and vibrant. The online experience wasn't.

The Insight

83% of Filipinos Buy Secondhand

Research confirmed what we already knew — the demand for thrift fashion in the Philippines is massive. Filipinos lead Southeast Asia in second-hand transactions. The buyers are there. A proper platform wasn't.

The Build

Born at Holy Angel University

A team of four computing students from HAU Angeles City set out to build the platform that the Philippine thrift community deserved. Angular on the frontend, MongoDB on the backend, deployed on Netlify — ThriftHub was built lean, fast, and focused.

Today

The Platform Filipinos Deserve

ThriftHub is live — a fully functional ukay-ukay marketplace with real product listings, size filtering, cart and checkout flows, and a growing community of thrift believers. This is just the beginning.

EARTH

For The Planet

Every purchase
is a protest.

Fast fashion is one of the world's largest polluters. The Philippine textile industry generates thousands of tons of waste annually — clothes that are discarded after just a few wears, fabrics that never decompose.

ThriftHub flips the script. When you buy second-hand, you're not just saving money — you're keeping a garment alive, reducing demand for new production, and choosing the planet over convenience.

What We Stand For

Our core values.

01

Transparency First

Every listing shows exactly what you're getting — real photos, real sizes, real conditions. No surprises, no hiding.

02

Built for Speed

From browse to checkout in minutes. We respect your time — no endless scrolling, no waiting for replies.

03

Sustainability is Non-Negotiable

Second-hand isn't a compromise — it's the smarter, greener, more responsible choice. We make it the obvious one.

04

Community-Driven

ThriftHub exists because of the Filipino thrift community. We build for buyers and sellers equally — this platform belongs to everyone.

05

Affordability for All

Great style shouldn't be gatekept behind brand-new prices. ThriftHub makes fashion accessible — starting at ₱199.

06

Quality Without Compromise

Every item on ThriftHub is reviewed before listing. Pre-loved doesn't mean low quality — it means pre-approved.
